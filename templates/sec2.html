factorization schematic ABCDisCo Fig 1
ABCD assumes the background density factorizes, p(NN1,NN2)=p(NN1)·p(NN2). Then B/D measures the NN1-ratio in the low-NN2 strip and transports C into A: N_A = N_B·N_C/N_D. Signal must localize in A.
signal & background planes PRD 112 092001 Figs 8b / 10b
The paper never sums these — signal MC (Simulation, normalised units) and background data (140 fb⁻¹, # vertices) carry different normalisations. Drawn side-by-side with a shared cut so A/B/C/D line up. Don't KDE the background: NN1 has point masses at exactly 0/1.
