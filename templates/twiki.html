CERN — official ABCD documentation

Primary references for the ABCD method and its likelihood (modified) form, as documented by the LHC collaborations and the statistics-tools maintainers. These are the canonical procedures the dashboard's diagnostics reconstruct.

Fitting tools (modified / likelihood ABCD)

TRooFit / TRooABCD — ATLASlogin may be required
twiki.cern.ch/twiki/bin/view/AtlasProtected/TRooFit
Buttinger's RooFit-based ABCD fitter. Walks the simultaneous fit with signal strength fixed at 0 vs. floated, plus validation-region non-closure as a systematic. The clearest procedural reference for the modified method.
RooStats
twiki.cern.ch/twiki/bin/view/RooStats/WebHome
The statistical toolkit underlying the likelihood ABCD fits — model building, profile likelihood, hypothesis tests.

Reinterpretation / data preservation

HEPData
hepdata.net
Published cell counts, closure tables, and per-region histograms behind ABCD analyses. Summary-level (not event-level) — the right source for validating ABCD arithmetic against known results.
Rivet
rivet.hepforge.org
Analysis-preservation routines often shipped alongside HEPData records for reinterpretation.
